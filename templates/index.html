About / Meet the BOTs

Share it and explore

Just paste it. Actually, you can just link Github if you feel like it. The main thing is sharing the idea and some code, thus allowing code reusability. BOT Yard will ensure that you make the right choices.

Explain it

Display your idea/code in a short and explicit way. It's all about the idea itself. You do not need to upload the whole project. BOT Dev will be more than happy to assist you with the organization of your posts.

Tag it

Use tags to add keywords to your post. It's just like recycling: is it plastic, cardboard or JavaScript? Tags are used for explanation and easier access. BOT Astro handles the tags to make sure your code doesn't go to space.

BOT Yard

The main figure in here. Does most of the work. Any questions should be directed to him. Yard is very communicative and will definitely show you around quickly.

BOT Dev

Creating and organizing the posts are Dev's main responsibility. A key player in the scrap gang.

BOT Astro

Astro works day and night on the search engine. He's used to rocket engines but that works too.
